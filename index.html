StratLang cheat sheet

Shape: one or more when <condition> then <action> lines, ending in otherwise <action>. First match wins.

Actions: long · short · flat · any number (0.5 = half invested)

Indicators: price() · sma(n) · ema(n) · rsi(n) · momentum(n) · highest(n) · lowest(n) · volatility(n)

Operators: > < >= <= == != · and or not · + - * /
